The pupose of this site is simple:

We're on a dangerous downslide. The United States once enjoyed the reputation of being a place where the rule of law was strong. Increasingly, we're a country where the wealthy and well connected succeed at the expense of the working class, using and abusing a fractured legal system to escape justice. This space exists to name names. It's important to have this information in a concise location to better analyze trends. You can quickly locate an elected official and see any allegations of corruption that have been levied against them.

This site uses three sources

It's impossible to find unbiased sources. It's difficult to even minimize bias. Many people belive these sites at least make an honest attempt, so they've been chosen.

A little about me:

My personal reasons for investing my time in this project are varied. I'm a software engineer, and this is the by-product of a few weeks spent learning the basics of the web framework Django. I have a strong interest in using data to bring about positive social change, and this is a step in that direction.


I also have a degree in political science and come partly from a background of working for various non-profits, political campaigns, and protest movements. This material is something I care deeply about. People who abuse their positions of status must be exposed and removed from power. If even one person reads this and decides to become involved or more engaged in our political process, then this has been time well spent.
